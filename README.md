<h2 align="center">Hey, I'm Aditya 👋</h2>

<p align="center">
  Backend engineer who likes to build things that don't break at 2am.
</p>

###

<p align="left">
I work on the Ngage platform at Comviva — designing backend services with Java and Spring Boot, building real-time data pipelines with Kafka, and owning the monitoring stack that keeps everything honest in production. My stack runs on Docker and Kubernetes, and I hold the bar on quality through JaCoCo coverage, Fortify scans, and clean dependency management with JFrog.<br><br>

Lately my scope has grown into platform reliability and AIOps — migrating our observability stack to Grafana and Prometheus, integrating PagerDuty and Statuspage for structured incident response, and building predictive alerting that catches issues before they escalate. I'm also doing R&D on ClickHouse as a replacement for Elasticsearch, and rewriting performance-critical modules in Rust where speed actually matters.<br><br>

Outside of work, I write on Medium, read about how other companies solve hard distributed systems problems, click photos, and work through a reading list that keeps getting longer.
</p>
